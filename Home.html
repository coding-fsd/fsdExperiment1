Presenting the new Mercedes-Benz C-Class.

Progressiveness meets luxury in the all-new Mercedes-Benz C-Class. And the design? An expression of sportiness. With the AVANTGARDE Exterior in the C 200 and C 220d variants, you can further enhance the individuality and sportiness of your vehicle, and show clearly that you attach importance to style and high quality.
